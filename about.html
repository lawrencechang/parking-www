LA Street Park


About

This is an early prototype of the Los Angeles Street Park service. The goal is to bring awareness of street parking regulations to a mobile device. This will enable drivers to quickly navigate to locations with legal parking, and thus waste less time driving around.

Why is this for New York City?

Currently this information is not available for the City of Los Angeles, but is for New York City. As part of the Challenge LA program, I hope to work with the city to collect and digitize this data. Once the data is collected, the code to map it has already been developed.
